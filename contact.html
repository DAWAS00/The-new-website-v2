Our Story

As a Computer Science student at Applied Science University, my journey with the Eman Platform began as a project inspired by Dr. Wareef. What started as an academic endeavor has evolved into a passionate mission to support student mental health. Together with my colleague Leen, who provided valuable insights and support throughout the development process, we recognized the critical need for accessible mental health resources in our academic community.

The platform's name, "Eman" (meaning "faith" in Arabic), reflects our belief in the power of support and understanding. Working on this project has shown us how technology can bridge the gap between students and mental health resources, making support more accessible to those who need it most.

Today, what began as a university project has grown into a dedicated platform aimed at creating a safe, supportive space for students to seek help, share their stories, and find the resources they need to thrive in their academic journey.
