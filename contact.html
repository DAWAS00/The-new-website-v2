My Story

As a Computer Science student at Applied Science University, my journey with the Eman Platform began as a project inspired by Dr. Wareef. What started as an academic endeavor has evolved into a passionate mission to support student mental health.

The platform's name, "Eman" (meaning "faith" in Arabic), reflects our belief in the power of support and understanding. Working on this project has shown me how technology can bridge the gap between students and mental health resources.

Today, what began as a university project has grown into a dedicated platform aimed at creating a safe, supportive space for students to seek help, share their stories, and find the resources they need to thrive in their academic journey.
