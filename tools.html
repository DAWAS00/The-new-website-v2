Self-Help Tools

Practical tools and techniques to support your mental well-being

Breathing Exercises

4-7-8 Breathing Technique

  1. Inhale quietly through your nose for 4 seconds
  2. Hold your breath for 7 seconds
  3. Exhale completely through your mouth for 8 seconds
  4. Repeat 4 times
Try Now

5-4-3-2-1 Grounding Exercise

When feeling anxious, identify:

  • 5 things you can see
  • 4 things you can touch
  • 3 things you can hear
  • 2 things you can smell
  • 1 thing you can taste

Quick Stress Relief

Try These Activities:

  • Progressive muscle relaxation
  • Quick stretching exercises
  • Mindful walking
  • Journaling prompts
  • Calming music playlist

Daily Affirmations

"I am capable of handling today's challenges"

"My feelings are valid, and it's okay to ask for help"

"I am growing stronger each day"

Study Break Timer

Use the Pomodoro Technique:

  • 25 minutes of focused study
  • 5 minutes break
  • Repeat 4 times
  • Take a longer 15-30 minute break
